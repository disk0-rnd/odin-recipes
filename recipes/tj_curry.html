Trader Joe's Easy Chicken Curry

Ingredients:

  • 1lb Chicken Breast, Cubed
  • 1 Bottle TJ's Sauce, Red or Yellow
  • 1 Yellow Onion, Diced
  • 2 Green Bell Peppers, Diced
  • 3 cloves Garlic diced
  • 1 Stub of Ginger diced

Cooking:

  1. Brown chicken in pain on all sides, 5-10 mins
  2. Remove chicken, set aside, add onions and bell peppers. Cook for 5 mins
  3. Add Garlic and Ginger, saute 2 mins
  4. Add chicken back to pot, add in bottle of curry mix
  5. Fill curry bottle with water/chicken broth, shake and add to pan
  6. Simmer for 30 minutes or until desired reduction
  7. Optional: Remove from heat, add 1cup heavy cream. Bring back to serving temperature.
